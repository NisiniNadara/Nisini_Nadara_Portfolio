Who I Am

I'm a computing student at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, majoring in Computer Science with minors in Mathematics and Physics. My work lives at the intersection of IoT, machine learning, and smart systems.

From building a Digital Twin-based predictive maintenance system for smart irrigation, to an Arduino-powered smart pedestrian crossing, to analysing network resilience across 30,000+ nodes — I gravitate toward projects that are both technically deep and practically meaningful.

I'm also a graphic designer with two years of experience at the Infocus Media Unit at Rajarata University, which means I care about how things look just as much as how they work.

Away from the screen, I'm a competitive chess player — Vice-Captain of the Rajarata University Women's Chess Team and a national award winner at SLUG 2023.
